Our foundational package. We will handle the core publishing mechanics, assign an ISBN, and get your manuscript ready for the world. Perfect for authors who have already completed their own editing and formatting.

A step up from Bronze, the Silver package includes our essential publishing services alongside professional copyediting. We will polish your prose, fix grammatical errors, and ensure your manuscript reads flawlessly.
